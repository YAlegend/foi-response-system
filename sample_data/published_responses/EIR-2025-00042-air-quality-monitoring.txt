Request for information under the Environmental Information Regulations 2004

Thank you for your request for information received on 6 January 2025, which has been considered under the Environmental Information Regulations 2004.

I can confirm that Hertfordshire County Council does hold the information you have requested. Please see our response to each of your questions below.

You asked:

1. How many air quality monitoring locations does the council operate, and where are they?
   Our response: The council operates nitrogen dioxide (NO2) diffusion tube monitoring at 96 locations across the county, concentrated in declared Air Quality Management Areas (AQMAs) in towns including Watford, St Albans, Hertford and Hitchin. A full list of monitoring locations with grid references is published in our annual Air Quality Status Report.

2. What were the most recent annual mean NO2 readings?
   Our response: For the 2024 monitoring year the annual mean NO2 concentration exceeded the national objective of 40 µg/m³ at 7 of the 96 locations, all on or adjacent to major road corridors. The county-wide average across all monitored sites was 26 µg/m³, continuing a downward trend since 2019.
