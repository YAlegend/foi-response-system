Request for information under the Freedom of Information Act 2000

Thank you for your request for information received on 28 April 2025, which has been considered under the Freedom of Information Act 2000.

I can confirm that Hertfordshire County Council does hold the information you have requested. Please see our response to each of your questions below.

You asked:

1. How many school admission appeals were lodged in the 2024 and 2025 admission cycles?
   Our response: The council received 1,510 admission appeals in the 2024 cycle and 1,420 in the 2025 cycle.

2. How many were upheld in favour of the parent?
   Our response: 395 appeals were upheld in 2024 and 360 in 2025.

3. Which phase saw the most appeals?
   Our response: Secondary transfer (Year 7) accounted for the majority of appeals in both years, reflecting pressure on preferred places at oversubscribed secondary schools.
