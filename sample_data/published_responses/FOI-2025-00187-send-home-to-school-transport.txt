Request for information under the Freedom of Information Act 2000

Thank you for your request for information received on 17 February 2025, which has been considered under the Freedom of Information Act 2000.

I can confirm that Hertfordshire County Council does hold the information you have requested. Please see our response to each of your questions below.

You asked:

1. What was the council's total spend on home-to-school transport for children with special educational needs and disabilities (SEND) in 2023/24 and 2024/25?
   Our response: The council's gross spend on SEND home-to-school transport was £38.6 million in 2023/24 and £42.1 million in 2024/25.

2. Please break this down by in-house provision and external/contracted provision.
   Our response: Of the 2024/25 total, £6.9 million related to in-house provision and £35.2 million related to externally contracted provision (taxis, minibuses and passenger assistants supplied under framework contracts).

3. How many pupils were transported in each year?
   Our response: An average of 4,950 SEND pupils were transported in 2023/24 and 5,210 in 2024/25.
