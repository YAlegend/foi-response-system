Request for information under the Freedom of Information Act 2000

Thank you for your request for information received on 14 January 2025, which has been considered under the Freedom of Information Act 2000.

You asked for the individual sickness absence records of three named senior officers.

Information withheld under section 40(2) of the Freedom of Information Act 2000
The information you have requested constitutes the personal data of identifiable individuals. Section 40(2) provides an exemption where disclosure would breach the data protection principles in the UK GDPR. Disclosing an identifiable individual's health and absence information would be unfair and would breach the first data protection principle (lawfulness, fairness and transparency). This is an absolute exemption and no public-interest test is required.

For transparency, the council does publish aggregate workforce sickness data — the average number of working days lost per full-time-equivalent employee — in its annual workforce report, and we would be happy to direct you to that published information.

Re-use of information
Information released under the Freedom of Information Act 2000 may only be re-used with the council's permission. Re-use without permission may breach copyright.
