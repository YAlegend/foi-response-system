Request for information under the Freedom of Information Act 2000

Thank you for your request for information received on 11 March 2025, which has been considered under the Freedom of Information Act 2000.

I can confirm that Hertfordshire County Council does hold the information you have requested. Please see our response to each of your questions below.

You asked:

1. How many potholes were reported across the county in the last 12 months?
   Our response: Between 1 March 2024 and 28 February 2025 the council logged 41,300 carriageway defect reports, of which 28,700 met the intervention criteria for repair.

2. What was the average time taken to repair a category 1 (most urgent) defect?
   Our response: Category 1 defects, which present an immediate or imminent hazard, are made safe within 24 hours of identification. In the period above the average time to make safe was 9.5 hours. Category 2 defects are repaired within 28 calendar days according to risk.

3. How much was spent on highway maintenance over the same period?
   Our response: The council spent £47.2 million on highway maintenance in 2024/25, including planned resurfacing, surface dressing and reactive pothole repair.
