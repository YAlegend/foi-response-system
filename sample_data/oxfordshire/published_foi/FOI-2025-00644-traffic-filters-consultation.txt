Request for information under the Freedom of Information Act 2000

Thank you for your request received on 21 January 2025, which has been considered under the Freedom of Information Act 2000.

I can confirm that Oxfordshire County Council does hold the information you have requested.

You asked:

1. How many responses did the council receive to its public consultation on the Oxford traffic filters?
   Our response: The public consultation on the proposed traffic filters received 5,683 responses through the council's consultation portal and by post and email.

2. What proportion of respondents supported the proposal?
   Our response: Of the responses received, approximately 42 per cent expressed support for the proposals, 48 per cent expressed opposition, and the remainder were neutral or did not express a clear view. The full consultation analysis report was published on the council's website.

3. When will the traffic filters be implemented?
   Our response: At the time of this response the traffic filters had not been implemented. Implementation has been linked to the completion of works on Oxford's railway bridges, which affect the road network the scheme relies upon. The council has stated that a trial would begin only after those works are sufficiently complete, and that the precise start date would be confirmed and publicised in advance.
